Project Lifecycle Flow

Detailed flow of how we manage the project lifecycle and collaborate with partners.

  1. 1. Registration

    Vendor / supplier onboarding, documents, pre-qualification.

  2. 2. Tendering

    Issue tenders, evaluate bids and shortlist partners.

  3. 3. Award

    Contract award and mobilization tracking.

  4. 4. Execution

    Site execution, quality checks and coordination.

  5. 5. Billing & AMC

    Billing, invoicing and post-delivery AMC tracking.

  6. 6. Help Desk & Analytics

    Support ticketing, SLA monitoring and analytics dashboards.
